    20”/10” Graphite pencil


    Based on a photograph taken of me and my sister on the day I was born, this drawing depicts our first moment together. Illustrating a portrait in pencil transforms a picture taken in one “click” into an intimate viewing of texture and contrast to appreciate the full significance of the moment. Brought into focus by the subjects’ surrounding negative space and imaginary and actual leading lines, me and my sister’s connection is displayed by her gentle touch on my forehead.

    Listen To Her Buzz

    7”/5” per panel Graphite pencil and coloured pencil


    In my life, my mom stands as the pinnacle role model of a strong female, inspiring me with her constant proactivity and reliability as a pillar of support. With intention to demonstrate my admiration of these qualities through my art, I found that a working bee serves as a relevant metaphor for the characteristics my mom holds. A bee is at times overlooked in its integral role in supporting the outdoor environment, yet by taking a closer inspection, we can appreciate all that it creates.
